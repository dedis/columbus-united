The project Columbus is a project aiming to facilitate the visualization of the blockchain of ByzCoin.
You can navigate through the different blocks by dragging right or left and by ajusting the zoom with scrollwheel. You can have more details about one block by clicking on it. If you need to use one of the hash value displayed in a tooltip, just click on it to copy it to your clipboard.
You can also select a transaction. You will see all instructions with their respective type (spawn, invoke or delete). A button is there to browse the whole blockchain in order to display the evolution of an instance throughout the blockchain.
